forecasting time series public policy

MSc Thesis — Forecasting Welfare & Poverty Impact in Ireland

Built ARIMA/SARIMA time series models in Python (Pandas, Statsmodels) using Ireland's Live Register data (2014–2025) to forecast welfare recipient trends through 2026 Q4. Estimated the impact of a €10 weekly welfare payment increase by modelling changes in aggregate and per-person income, applying income elasticity of poverty to project reductions in national poverty rates. Developed interactive Plotly dashboards to communicate welfare trends and policy insights clearly to non-technical stakeholders.

  • Models ARIMA · SARIMA
  • Data Ireland Live Register, 2014–2025
  • Tools Python · Pandas · Statsmodels · Plotly
